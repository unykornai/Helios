THE STRUCTURE

How the money flows down

Think of it like a chain. The person at the top gets paid from everything below them. The person closest to the action makes the most. Everyone in between earns based on distance. Simple.

┌─ POSITION 1 — The person at the top. Gets paid from everyone below.
│  └─ POSITION 2 — Under Position 1. Gets paid from everything under them.
│     └─ POSITION 3 — Did the work. Introduced the members. Receives the most.
│         └─ Level 1 — 5 people   (Position 3's directs)
│             └─ Level 2 — 25 people
│                 └─ Level 3 — 125 people
│                     └─ Level 4 — 625 people
│                         └─ Level 5 — 3,125 people
│                             └─ Level 6 — 15,625 people
│                                 └─ Level 7+ — 30,095+ people
                                     ... through Level 15
Key point: Position 3 did the work and earns the most. Position 2 earns from everything under them — without introducing anyone. Position 1 earns from the entire network. The further up you are, the more levels of activity pay you — but the per-member allocation is smaller because of distance. That's the formula. Fair and predictable.
