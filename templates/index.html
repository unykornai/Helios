How the Protocol Operates

Helios employs a deterministic settlement model. Every membership generates a fixed allocation distributed through distance-weighted tiers. No discretionary payouts. No manual intervention.

Direct Referral Settlement

$22.50 per qualified referral

When a new member is activated through your unique referral identifier, a settlement of $22.50 is allocated to you immediately. This is a fixed, protocol-enforced distribution.

Distance-Weighted Allocation

15 tiers of settlement depth

Allocations extend through 15 connection tiers, weighted by distance from your position. Tier 1 receives the highest weighting; each subsequent tier decays by a factor of two.

Asset-Backed Reserve

Physical precious metals from APMEX

A portion of every membership is allocated to the acquisition of physical gold and silver through APMEX. Certificates are redeemable against verified metal holdings.

Membership Process

Three steps to activate your position in the protocol.

1

Establish Membership

A single $100 membership fee is allocated deterministically: $45 to the settlement pool, $22.50 to your referring member, $15 to the precious metals reserve, $10 to liquidity, and $10 to protocol infrastructure. Every dollar is accounted for.

2

Refer Qualified Members

Each member is allocated five referral positions. When a referred individual completes their membership, $22.50 is settled to you immediately. Five referrals yield $112.50 in direct settlement.

3

Receive Tiered Settlements

As your referred members refer others, settlement continues through 15 tiers of depth. Allocation is weighted by distance — tier 1 at 50%, tier 2 at 25%, and so on. Settlements are protocol-enforced and automatic.

Asset-Backed Reserves

A defined portion of every membership is allocated to the acquisition of physical precious metals through APMEX, one of the world's largest accredited dealers. All holdings are verified on-chain via XRPL with SHA-256 proof of custody.
