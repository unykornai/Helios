How Capital Flows Through the Protocol

Every dollar follows a published, deterministic path. Fiat enters, stablecoins settle, gold is acquired, certificates are issued, and value is allocated across 15 levels. No discretionary intervention. No manual payouts.

1

Member Joins

A $100 membership fee is submitted. Every dollar is allocated according to published percentages — no discretionary funds exist.

2

Fiat → Stablecoin

Incoming fiat is converted to USDC/USDT on XRPL or Stellar. Stablecoins provide transparent, auditable settlement rails.

3

Stablecoin → Physical Gold

Stablecoins are used to purchase physical gold by weight through APMEX, one of the world's largest accredited precious metals dealers.

4

Gold → Digital Certificates

Gold is tokenized into Gold Certificates (NFTs), Bonus Certificates, and Staking Certificates. Each represents a verifiable weight of physical gold.

5

Allocation Across 15 Levels

The deterministic weighting model distributes gold-backed value across up to 15 levels of the contribution network. Fixed math. No discretion.

Web3 Settlement Infrastructure

  • Native NFT support for certificates

Stellar

Gold-Backed Digital Certificates

All value within the protocol is represented by digital certificates — each backed by a verifiable weight of physical gold, issued as NFTs on XRPL.

Gold Certificates

Primary Value Instrument

Represent allocated gold weight. Redeemable for physical metal from APMEX or convertible to stablecoin. Each certificate carries a SHA-256 proof of custody anchored on XRPL.

Bonus Certificates

Activity-Based Allocation

Issued at enrollment (Joining Bonus), upon direct referral activation (Referral Bonus), and based on participation metrics (Activity Bonus). Fixed rules, no discretion.

Staking Certificates

Lock Duration Rewards

Members may lock certificates for a defined period to receive additional allocation bonuses. Longer lock durations yield higher bonus multipliers. All terms are protocol-enforced.
